The Queen

Periodt.

Mother is a Queen of Queens. Words cannot describe how amazing this woman is. She has been 21 for longer than I have been alive. This alone is an astonishing feat. With 800 children, she remains slim by being constantly on the move. With her trust horse archibelt, she travels far and wide taking care of the past queens of the land. Mother is always someone you can trust and never fails her people. With years of raising children under her belt, she has raised a family that will change the world.
